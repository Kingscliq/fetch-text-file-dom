T&oslash;rres Jonsson was born to Jon Mikkjelsson (2111.) and Marita Eriksdotter (2112.) at
Hauge under Holmedal (236/2) in Holmedal Parish March 21, 1750. He married Marita
Larsdotter July 15, 1775, and fathered Lars T&oslash;rresson (21.). He held the lease on husmannplass
Sanden (248/14) on Hauge under Holmedal from around 1797 to 1822. He died January 1, 1826.
