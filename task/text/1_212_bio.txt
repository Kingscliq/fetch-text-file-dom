Marita Larsdotter was born to Lars Knudsson (2121.) and Guri Larsdotter (2122.) at Sunde
(258) in Holmedal Parish April 15, 1752. She married T&oslash;rres Jonsson (211.) July 15, 1775, and
mothered Lars T&oslash;rresson (21.). They resided at husmannplass Sanden (248/14) on Hauge under
Holmedal in Holmedal Parish. She died March 18, 1802.
